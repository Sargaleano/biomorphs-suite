About Biomorph Creator

What is this?

The Biomorph Creator is a companion to the Biomorph Evolver. Sculpt a single creature manually by adjusting its genes with sliders, rather than evolving a population. Craft a precise starting creature, then seed it into the Evolver for further evolution.

Gene modes

  • Friendly — 5 intuitive parameters that map to the full 9-gene system internally: Complexity (recursion depth), Spread (horizontal fan), Height (vertical scale), Taper (branch shortening toward tips), Sprawl (horizontal extremities).
  • Original — direct access to all 9 Dawkins genes (g0–g7 + recursion depth), giving full control over the creature's geometry.

The genome display at the bottom always shows the active values and, in friendly mode, the expanded 9-gene equivalent.

Controls

  • 🎲 Random — jumps to a random genome in the current mode.
  • ✴️ Reset — returns to the archetype genome from The Blind Watchmaker.
  • ⏬ Export biomorph — saves the current creature as a biomorph-creator-export.json file, ready to import into the Evolver's Gallery via ⏫.
  • 📸 Screenshot — saves the biomorph as a biomorph-creator.png image.
  • 👽 Open Evolver — opens the Biomorph Evolver in a new tab.

Using with the Evolver

1. Design your creature here and click ⏬ Export biomorph.
2. Open the Evolver (or click 👽), switch to the ⭐ Gallery tab, and click ⏫ to load the file.
3. Your creature appears in the gallery — click it to set it as parent and start evolving.
